Reporting a Hazardous Materials Incident

Reporting a Hazardous Materials Incident
Your first step in reporting a Hazardous Materials incident is to call 911. Please have as much of the following information as possible:
  • Identity of the caller
  • Location, date and time of spill, release, or threatened release
  • Location of the threatened or involved waterway or storm drains
  • The substance, quantity involved, and isotope, if necessary
  • The chemical name (if known, it should be reported if the chemical is extremely hazardous)
  • A description of what happened
