resolution a resolution for the transfer of funds for the rainy day fund whereas the common council for the city of by ordinance did create a rainy day fund in both the general fund fund number and the motor vehicle fund fund number and whereas the common council for the city of finds it necessary to transfer and expend the total sum of two hundred ninety six thousand three hundred forty five dollars and twenty three cents from the two rainy day funds for the purpose of improving sycamore street now therefore be it resolved that the sum of one hundred ninety six thousand three hundred forty five dollars and twenty three cents shall be transferred from the general rainy day fund fund number to the general fund fund be it further resolved that the sum of one hundred thousand dollars shall be transferred from the motor vehicle rainy day fund fund to the motor vehicle fund fund this resolution is approved and adopted on the day of craft hall
