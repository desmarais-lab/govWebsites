ADMINISTRATION AND FINANCE COMMITTEE
The Administration and Finance Committee of the City-County Council met on Monday,
October 12, 2015. Chair Angela Mansfield called the meeting to order at 5:29 p.m. with
the following members present: Pamela Hickman, Maggie Lewis, Marilyn Pfisterer,
Jack Sandlin and Joseph Simpson. Mary Moriarty Adams arrived shortly thereafter.
Benjamin Hunter was absent. General Counsel Fred Biesecker, Chief Financial Officer
(CFO) Bart Brown, and Fiscal Analyst Ryan Kramer represented Council staff.
Chair Mansfield asked committee members to introduce themselves and indicate which
portion of the County they represent.
BUDGET HEARING
PROPOSAL NO. 265, 2015 - adopts the annual budget for the City of Indianapolis and
Marion County for 2016
Chair Mansfield stated that a meeting is usually scheduled in case there are any lastminute amendments needed before the full budget passage.
{Clerk’s Note: Councillor Moriarty Adams arrived at 5:30 p.m.}
Matthew Kimmick, City Controller, Office of Finance and Management (OFM), said that
they only have one additional amendment to offer. He provided a handout and said that
the amendment would add the “National College of Probate Judges” as an addition to
the list of authorized memberships for the Judiciary branch. He said that a list of
approved organization memberships/subscriptions are included in the budget
ordinance, as a resource for further education.
Councillor Sandlin moved, seconded by Councillor Pfisterer, to “Amend” Proposal No.
265, 2015 by adding the “National College of Probate Judges” to the Judiciary’s
authorized memberships list. The motion carried by a vote of 7-0.
Councillor Pfisterer said that she would like to be noted as a co-sponsor on the
amendment. She moved, seconded by Councillor Moriarty Adams, to send Proposal
No. 265, 2015 to the full Council with a “Do Pass As Amended” recommendation. The
motion carried by a vote of 7-0.
There being no further business, and upon motion duly made, the meeting was
adjourned at 5:32 p.m.
