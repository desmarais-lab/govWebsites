MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS COMMITTEE
The Municipal Corporations Committee of the City-County Council met on Monday,
November 29, 2004. Chair Ron Gibson called the meeting to order at 4:38 p.m. with the
following members present: Greg Bowes, Lonnell Conley, Sherron Franklin, Scott Keller, Isaac
Randolph, and Earl Salisbury.
PROPOSAL NO. 717, 2004 - reappoints Rozelle Boyd to the Capital Improvement Board
Chair Gibson explained that President Boyd was attending another meeting inside the building
and would not be available for attendance this afternoon.
He added that there is a technical amendment to be made to Proposal No. 717, 2004.
Specifically, this is a 2-year appointment, and the term ending December 31, 2005 as stated on
the ordinance, should be changed to January 14, 2006.
Councillor Bowes stated that he has extensively researched this, and he is comfortable that this
date meets legal requirements.
Councillor Randolph moved, seconded by Councillor Conley, to make this technical
amendment. This motion carried by a vote of 7-0. Councillor Conley moved, seconded by
Councillor Randolph, to send Proposal No. 717, 2004 to the full Council with a “Do Pass As
Amended” recommendation. This motion carried by a vote of 7-0.
PROPOSAL NO. 736, 2004 - reappoints Robert Pfeifer to the Health and Hospital Corporation
Board of Trustees
Matt Gutwein, Executive Director, Health and Hospital Corporation (H&H), was present in
support of Mr. Pfeifer. He spoke in high regards of Mr. Pfeifer and encouraged the Committee
to reappoint Mr. Pfeifer. He added that Mr. Pfeifer has perfect attendance.
Mr. Pfeifer stated that he enjoys serving on the Board and is happy to participate. He thanked
the Committee for another nomination.
Councillor Randolph moved, seconded by Councillor Conley, to send Proposal No. 736, 2004 to
the full Council with a “Do Pass” recommendation. This motion carried by a vote of 7-0.
With no further business pending, and upon motion duly made, the Municipal Corporations
Committee of the City-County Council was adjourned at 4:54 p.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Ron Gibson, Chair
