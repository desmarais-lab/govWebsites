****** Board_of_Works_&_Safety_&#8211;_February_19,_2013 ******
    * Published on February 19, 2013
    * under Board_of_Works, News

The Board of Works and Safety for the City of Attica met, pursuant to Public
Notice, on Wednesday, February 19 at 4:30 P.M. in the City Council Room.
Present were Mayor, Robert Shepherd, City Clerk, Susan Stoll and Board Member,
Duane Roderick. Also present was City Attorney, Tom O’Connor.
**** APPROVAL OF MINUTES ****
Minutes of the regular session held on February 5, 2013 were approved as
prepared on a motion by Duane Roderick, seconded by Mayor Shepherd.
**** CITIZENS PETITIONS ****
There were no citizens petitions to be presented to the Board.
**** DEPARTMENT HEADS REPORTS ****
Mr. Jean reported that work continues to be done on the sewer lines on a
section of McDonald St.
Mr. O’Farrell reported that he had completed inventory for year-end
reporting. The tanker will be worked on in March.
Mr. Dismore spoke about the McDonald’s restaurant renovation.
**** UNFINISHED BUSINESS ****
There was no unfinished business presented.
**** NEW BUSINESS ****
There was no new business presented.
**** CLAIMS ****
Claims dated on February 19, 2013 for $ 284,353.06, as shown by the Allowance
of Accounts Payable Vouchers, were presented to the board. Mr. Roderick made a
motion to pay the claims, seconded by Mayor Shepherd.
**** MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS ****
There was no miscellaneous business to discuss.
There being no further business to be brought before the board; the meeting was
adjourned.
