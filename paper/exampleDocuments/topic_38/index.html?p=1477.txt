****** Board_of_Works_&_Safety_&#8211;_February_21,_2017 ******
    * Published on March 16, 2017
    * under Board_of_Works, News

**** Call to Order ****
The Board of Works and Safety for the City of Attica met, pursuant to Public
Notice, on Thursday, February 9, 2017, 4:30 P.M. in the City Council Room.
**** Roll Call ****
Present were Mayor Roderick, Board Members, Mr. Smart and Mr. Gayler, City
Attorney, Tom O’Connor and Clerk-Treasurer, Susan Stoll.
**** Approval of Minutes ****
Upon Motion duly made by Mr. Gayler, seconded by Mr. Smart and adopted, Minutes
from the previous Board of Works Meeting for February 9, 2017 were approved as
written.
**** Citizens Petitions ****
There were no citizen’s petitions to be heard.
**** Department Head Reports ****
Ron Jean, Street and Water Supervisor was present with nothing to report.
Police Chief, Bob Cole was present. He reported that Alden Hohenstein, our new
officer, started on Friday, February 17th. Another officer hit a deer while
being called out on Sunday, February 19th. The vehicle was damaged and is
currently at a body shop for repairs.
Fire Chief, Ed O’Farrell was not present.
Jeff Pycke, the Building Commissioner was present with nothing to report.
**** Unfinished Business ****
There was none.
**** New Business ****
Utility Credits: Mr. Gayler made a motion to approve, Mr. Smart seconded, the
motion was passed.
Claims: Mr. Smart made a motion to approve, Mr. Gayler seconded, the motion was
passed.
**** Miscellaneous Business ****
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 4:33 pm. The next
Board of Works meeting will be held on Tuesday, March 7, 2017 at 4:30 pm.
