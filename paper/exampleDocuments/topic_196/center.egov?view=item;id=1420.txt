STORMWATER MANAGEMENT BOARD OF THE CITY OF JASPER
August 8, 2016
The Stormwater Management Board of the City of Jasper, Indiana, met in regular session on
August 8, 2016 in the Council Chambers of City Hall located at 610 Main Street.

APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES
The Board members examined the minutes of the regular meeting that was held on July 11, 2016.

STORMWATER DEPARTMENT REPORTS by City Engineer Chad Hurm
College View Estates Project City Engineer Chad Hurm stated that the entire storm sewer is
installed at the College View Estates project. Chad Hurm also stated the contractor currently has
some patching, grade work, and curb work and should then be complete.
Golden Acres Phase Two Project City Engineer Chad Hurm stated that all of the stormsewer
is installed at Golden Acres Phase Two project. Chad Hurm also stated additional pipe had to be

Drainage Issue on Newton Street
City Engineer Chad Hurm and the Board discussed a
drainage problem on Newton Street. It appears that INDOT repaved the road without milling
down and covered a good portion of the storm inlets. Roger Messmer stated he was going to set
up a meeting to further discuss this issue.
Golden Acres Phase Two Project City Engineer Chad Hurm stated that, by mistake, Love
Excavating was not awarded the low bid for the Golden Acres Phase Two project. Chad Hurm
requested to accept the lowest bid from Love Excavating for $170,325.31.
Secretary Krodel motioned to approve the bid for Love Excavation in the amount of
$170,325.31. Vice Chairman Schmidt seconded the motion. Motion passed 3-0.
