STORMWATER MANAGEMENT BOARD OF THE CITY OF JASPER
May 11, 2015
The Stormwater Management Board of the City of Jasper, Indiana, met in regular session on
May 11, 2015 in the Council Chambers of City Hall located at 610 Main Street.

Chairman Roger Messmer, who presided, called the meeting to order at 8:00 a.m.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE
Chairman Roger Messmer led the Pledge of Allegiance.
APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES
The Board members examined the minutes of the regular meeting that was held on April
13, 2015. Chairman Messmer motioned and Secretary Krodel seconded to approve the minutes
as presented. Motion carried 3-0.
CLAIMS
Chairman Messmer presented claims in the amount of $30,011.53 from April 10, 2015, through
May 7, 2015.
Vice Chairman Schmidt motioned to approve the claims as presented. Secretary Krodel
seconded. Motion passed 3-0.

Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy presented to the Board a copy of the
April 2015 Building Permits, with 11 additional ERUs indicated on the list.

IDEM Monthly Report
Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated that the monthly
IDEM report has been completed and filed. The report listed 16 sites that were inspected in the
month of April. There were five rain events over ½” of accumulation last month.
Stormwater Violations
Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated that there were no
violations since the last Board meeting.
Illicit Discharge Update Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated a new illicit discharge
was found at 1879 3rd Street. White paint was found in a grass swale just outside of a home
owned by Terry Egnew. Mr. Egnew agree to fix the problem and call for a follow up inspection.
Chad Mundy presented to the Board pictures of the illicit sink in the basement.
Drainage Permits
Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated that there were eight new
drainage permits since last month’s Storm Water Board meeting. There are also seven open
drainage permits.
.
Erosion Control Permits Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated that there was one new
permit issued for the Memorial Hospital parking lot project. There was one new plan summited
to the City for the New Brighton Properties project.
Memorial Hospital Project Drainage Meeting
Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy
stated several employees of the City of Jasper had a meeting with Richard Berger, BSA Life
Structures and several employees of Memorial Hospital. This meeting was to talk about
ownership of storm structures on Memorial Hospital’s property. A combination of planning and
working together will be required to document ownership of storm structures. It was also
determined that building permits and Rule 5 permits can be issued.
Meeting with Kerstiens Homes & Designs Stormwater Coordinator Chad Mundy stated that
several employees of the City of Jasper had a meeting with Todd Kerstiens and Al Mehringer,
both of Kerstiens Homes & Designs. This meeting was to talk about several stormwater taps that
are not to the City’s specifications in Brookstone IV. Todd Kerstiens agreed to make the
corrective actions.
ADJOURNMENT
There being no further business to come before the Board, a motion was made by Secretary
Krodel, seconded by Vice Chairman Schmidt and carried 3-0 to adjourn the meeting. The
meeting adjourned at 9:09 a.m.
