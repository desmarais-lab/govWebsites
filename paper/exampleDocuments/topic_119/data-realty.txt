****** Data Realty ******

Data Realty is a data management company operating data centers that
specializes in providing data solutions that allow companies to discover
actionable insights from disparate data sources. There are plenty of facilities
that can simply store your data, but Data Realty helps bring data to life by
uncovering opportunities. Because they anticipated the need for businesses to
better analyze their data, their data centers and data management platforms are
designed in collaboration with world-renowned data scientists specifically for
the creation of new, unique data sets to be used for analysis. Data Realty help
clients realize a "Return on Data," where a former cost center is transformed
into a revenue-generator.
