Moving Here
Moving to Madison

If you have moved to Madison recently or are thinking of moving to Madison, we
want to say WELCOME!

We're so glad you chose Madison to be your home. We know it is a great place to
live, work, and play, as well as start or expand a business. We've made some
handy documents for your use as you get to know our community. Please see the
documents below for information. However, if these don't answer your questions,
give us a call at 812-265-8300 or visit the Madison_Area_Chamber_of_Commerce
for a welcome packet!
