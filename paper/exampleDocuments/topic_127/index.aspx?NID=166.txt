Incident Reports
Incident Reports
Copies of police case (incident) reports made by a Hobart Police Officer may be
obtained through the Records Division of the Hobart Police Department at 705 E.
4th Street, Hobart, IN 46342, phone 219-942-1125 (option 2). 

Numerous police reports are made each day, so the more specific you can be in
your request (providing the date, time, location, type of incident, names of
people involved, etc.), the more likely it is the correct report will be found.
If you have the case number, please reference it. The cost of a incident report
is $6.00.  Payment type of cash or check only.
