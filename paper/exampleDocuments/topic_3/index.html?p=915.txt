****** Senior_citizens_reminded_to_sign_up_for_Safer_Seniors_and_Neighbors
Helping_Neighbors ******
    * Published on May 14, 2012
    * under Fire_Department_Updates, News, Police_Department_Updates

The City’s Fire and Police Departments continue to offer their Safer Seniors
Program to assist senior citizens and those physically challenged with home
evaluations for fire and safety hazards. Appointments are free and can be made
for Thursday afternoons between 1:00 &#8211; 3:00 p.m. by calling the Fire
Department at 654-4329.
The City’s Department of Building Services and Parks and Streets Department
continue to offer their Neighbors Helping Neighbors Program, providing senior
citizens and physically challenged residents with evaluations of yard and
exterior home conditions. A volunteer base of fellow Frankfort residents are
contacted to assist homeowners with identified yard work and exterior repairs.
Appointments are free and can be made for Tuesday afternoons between 1:00 –
3:00 p.m. by calling the Department of Building Services at 654-5278.
City staff who conduct the home visits for both programs provide homeowners
with safety checklists with the results of their evaluations. Call and make an
appointment today!
