Board of Works & Safety – January 17, 2017
Published on
February 23, 2017
under
Board of Works
, 
News
Call to Order
The Board of Works and Safety for the City of Attica met, pursuant to Public Notice, on Tuesday, January 17, 2017, 4:30 P.M. in the City Council Room.
Roll Call
Present were Mayor Roderick and Board Members, Mr. Smart and Mr. Gayler. Also present were City Attorney, Thomas P. O’Connor and Clerk-Treasurer, Susan Stoll.
Approval of Minutes
Upon Motion duly made by Mr. Smart, seconded by Mr. Gayler and adopted, Minutes from the previous Board of Works Meeting for January 3, 2017 were approved as written.
Citizens Petitions
There were no citizen’s petitions to be heard.
Department Head Reports
Ron Jean, Street and Water Supervisor was present with nothing to report.
Police Chief, Bob Cole was present; he reported that the APD made 5003 calls in the City of Attica in 2016. The APD made 6 arrests of drug dealers in the last month.   A pursuit out of Terre Haute traveled through Attica on Sunday and the vehicle was stopped in Attica by the APD. Officer Pearman was attacked by an impaired driver in another incident.
Fire Chief, Ed O’Farrell was not present.
Jeff Pycke, the Building Commissioner was present. He reported he has sent a letter to Tom Farley inquiring about his intentions to improve the appearance of his building on N. Perry St. in downtown. Tom O’Connor informed the Board that the property that is owned by Darling LLC, 215 S. Perry St., is changing ownership. The new owner may come to the City asking for additional time to get repairs/improvements done.
Unfinished Business
There was none.
New Business
Utility Credits; Mr. Smart made a motion to approve, Mr. Gayler seconded, the motion was passed.
Claims; Mr. Smart made a motion to approve, Mr. Gayler seconded, the motion was passed.
Adjournment
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 4:37 pm. The next Board of Works meeting will be held on Tuesday, February 7, 2017 at 4:30 pm.
