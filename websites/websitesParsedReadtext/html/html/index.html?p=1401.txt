Board of Works & Safety – December 6, 2016
Published on
January 16, 2017
under
Board of Works
, 
News
Call to Order
The Board of Works and Safety for the City of Attica met, pursuant to Public Notice, on Tuesday, December 6, 2016, 4:30 P.M. in the City Council Room.
Roll Call
Present was Mayor Roderick and Board Member, Mr. Smart. Also present was City Attorney, Thomas P. O’Connor and Clerk-Treasurer, Susan Stoll.
Approval of Minutes
Upon Motion duly made, seconded and adopted, Minutes from the previous Board of Works Meeting for November 15, 2016 were approved as written.
Citizens Petitions
There were no citizens petitions to be heard.
Department Head Reports
Ron Jean, Street and Water Supervisor, reported that the leaf pickup is going well.
Police Chief Bob Cole was present with nothing to report.
Fire Chief, Ed O’Farrell was not present
Jeff Pyke, the Building Commissioner, reported that he sent a letter to the owner of 1312 Ivy Lane 2 months ago, requesting that repairs be done. This notice had a deadline for action of 11/20/2016. Mr. O’Connor needs to follow up on the lack of action. The downtown addresses that received a similar request for repairs have also met the stated deadline for action.
Unfinished Business
There was none.
New Business
Utility Credits; Mr. Smart made a motion to approve, Mr. Roderick seconded. The motion passed.
Upon Motion duly made and carried, the claims, as presented, were approved.
Miscellaneous Business
Mayor Roderick reported on Dec. 11, 2016 at 1:00 pm there will be a Flag Ceremony at the City Building to commemorate the 200
th
 anniversary of the State of Indiana.
Adjournment
There being no further business, Mr. Smart made a motion to adjourn. Mr. Roderick seconded and the meeting was adjourned at 4:55 P.M. The next Board of Works meeting will be held on December 20, 2016 at 4:30 pm.
